Global Ads Analytics Dashboard

Executive Summary
This project focuses on building an interactive analytics dashboard to evaluate advertising performance across multiple campaigns and channels.
The goal was to create a unified view of ad performance so that key metrics such as impressions, clicks, conversions, and return on ad spend (ROAS) can be analyzed in one place. Instead of reviewing scattered reports, this dashboard enables quick identification of high-performing campaigns and inefficient ad spend.
The project reflects a real-world marketing analytics scenario where businesses need to monitor performance and optimize decisions based on data.

Business Problem
Digital advertising campaigns are often managed across multiple platforms, making it difficult to track performance effectively.
Common challenges include:
Fragmented data across different channels
Difficulty comparing campaign performance
Lack of visibility into ROI and ad spend efficiency
Time-consuming manual reporting
Without a centralized dashboard, marketing decisions become slow and less accurate.
This project solves that by providing a unified performance view.

Methodology
Data collection from advertising campaign datasets
Data cleaning and transformation
KPI calculation (CTR, CPC, ROAS, conversions)
Dashboard design and visualization
Interactive filtering for deeper analysis

Key Metrics
Impressions (visibility)
Clicks (engagement)
Click-Through Rate (CTR)
Cost Per Click (CPC)
Conversions
Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
Total Ad Spend

These metrics are standard for evaluating advertising effectiveness and budget efficiency

Key Insights
A small number of campaigns contribute significantly to total conversions
Some high-spend campaigns generate low returns, indicating inefficient budget allocation
Engagement varies across channels, with certain platforms delivering stronger CTR
Performance differs across regions and audience segments
Time-based trends show peak performance periods and underperforming intervals

Business Impact
Provides a centralized view of advertising performance across campaigns
Helps identify high-performing and underperforming ads quickly
Enables more efficient budget allocation based on ROI
Reduces manual reporting effort and improves decision speed

In real-world systems, unified dashboards significantly reduce reporting complexity and improve decision-making speed

Business Recommendations
Reallocate budget toward campaigns with higher ROAS
Pause or optimize low-performing campaigns
Focus on high-engagement channels for better conversion
Use time-based trends to schedule campaigns strategically
Continuously monitor KPIs to improve performance
